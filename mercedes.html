Mercedes-Benz-A-Class

Price: R784 554

Speed: 270km/h.

Description

An important new Mercedes-Benz A-Class variant will be the A 250 e. This combines a 1.3-litre petrol engine with a punchy electric motor for total system power of 218PS. It does 0-62mph in 7.4 seconds and, thanks to the 15.6kWh plug-in battery, is capable of travelling up to 50 miles in pure electric guise.
